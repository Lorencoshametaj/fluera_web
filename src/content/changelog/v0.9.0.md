Ghost Map ships in its first usable form. After Step 3 of the cycle, students can trigger a comparison overlay: their canvas versus an AI-generated ideal map. Mismatches pulse visually.

**New**

- Ghost Map overlay with red (missing), yellow (mis-linked), blue (added) cues
- Confidence slider on every Socratic reveal — primes hypercorrection
- On-canvas attempt flow: students write the correction by hand before the ideal reveals
- FAB with Confronta/Rivela buttons during attempts

**Improved**

- Gemini positioning is non-deterministic; we added an anti-overlap post-pass (350px min spacing)
- Drawing pipeline no longer blocks during inline attempt
- Label text is now anchored at fixed screen size (160px, 1 line); rectangles scale with canvas

**Known issues**

- Gemini occasionally bunches nodes on one cluster — the post-pass sometimes pushes them far from related groups
- No auto-detect for when a student finishes writing an attempt (manual confirm only)
- Visual polish on ghost nodes (animations, transitions) will improve in v0.10
